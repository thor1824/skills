## What to build

Build the weighted voting slice so the acting **Team member** can submit one **Vote** per **Lunch poll** as an ordered ranking of one to three distinct **Restaurants**, and later replace that ranking while the **Lunch poll** remains open. The app should enforce the weighted voting invariants end-to-end and surface the acting member's current **Vote** for editing.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] The acting **Team member** can submit a **Vote** ranking one, two, or three distinct **Restaurants** in order
- [ ] The app rejects duplicate **Restaurants** in a **Vote** and rejects rankings longer than three choices
- [ ] A **Team member** can have at most one **Vote** per **Lunch poll**
- [ ] When the acting **Team member** updates an existing **Vote**, the new ranking replaces the previous ranking instead of accumulating with it
- [ ] The app shows the acting **Team member** their current **Vote** while the **Lunch poll** is open
